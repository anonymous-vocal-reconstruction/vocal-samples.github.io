Because our simulator is fully differentiable, we can backpropagate the area function through a GAN trained on vocal MRI data to synthesize dynamic vocal tract movements. However, the sound quality is lower than backpropagating through only an area function. This is because the GAN is trained on only 75 speakers, and the MRI data is noisy and low resolution.
